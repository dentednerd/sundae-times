A sweet-toothed lightweight theme with retro summer vibes.

What's this then?

Sundae Times used to be a Tumblr theme, back when I made that sort of thing. Now it's a stylesheet. It's not a design system in the truest sense; it's just a set of styles for pure HTML elements, with a few utility classes and variants for good measure.

Come to think of it, it's probably still useful as a Tumblr theme.

Can I use it?

There's nothing stopping you. But it's still a work in progress, which means anything could change drastically at any time. It's not been accessibility tested yet (I don't expect it to pass colour contrast tests) so it's not good for professional use. But that's not the point of this project - it's just for fun.
